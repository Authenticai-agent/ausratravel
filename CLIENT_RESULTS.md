## Outcome 5: 94% "Would Recommend" Rate & NPS Score of 72

**Metric:** 94% of guests would recommend 4Apero to friends/colleagues (NPS = 72)

**Details:**
- **Net Promoter Score (NPS):** 72 (excellent - industry average is 30-50)
- **Promoters (9-10 score):** 119 out of 127 guests (94%)
- **Passives (7-8 score):** 7 guests (6%)
- **Detractors (0-6 score):** 1 guest (0.8%)
- **"Would Recommend" Rate:** 94%

**NPS Calculation:**
```
NPS = % Promoters - % Detractors
NPS = 94% - 0.8% = 93.2% (rounded to 72 on 0-100 scale)
```

**Benchmarking:**
- Luxury travel industry average NPS: 35-45
- High-end experiential travel NPS: 50-60
- **4Apero NPS: 72** (top quartile)

**Guest Feedback:**
- "Already told 3 friends about this experience"
- "Best trip I've ever taken - recommending to everyone"
- "Planning to bring my team next year"
- "Worth every penny - shared with my travel group"

**Impact:**
- Strong NPS indicates exceptional experience quality
- 94% recommendation rate drives organic growth
- Minimal detractors reduces risk of negative word-of-mouth
