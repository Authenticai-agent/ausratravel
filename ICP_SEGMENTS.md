# Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) Segments & Dream Accounts

## ICP Segment 1: Corporate Retreat & Team Building Planners

### Titles:
- Director of People Operations / HR
- Head of Employee Experience
- VP of Talent & Culture
- Corporate Events Manager
- Chief People Officer (CPO)
- Employee Engagement Director
- Team Building Coordinator
- Executive Retreat Planner
- Internal Communications Director

### Firmographics:
- **Company Size:** 200-5,000 employees
- **Industry:** Technology, Consulting, Finance, Legal, Healthcare, Professional Services
- **Revenue:** $50M - $2B annually
- **Location:** North America (US, Canada), UK, Western Europe
- **Budget Authority:** $50K - $500K per year for offsites/retreats
- **Team Size:** 8-12 people per retreat (matches our capacity)
- **Frequency:** Quarterly or annual team-building retreats
- **Pain Points:** Need unique, authentic experiences beyond typical corporate venues; desire for team bonding in inspiring settings; want all-inclusive, turnkey solutions

### Value Proposition:
- Authentic French artisan experiences create memorable team bonding
- Small groups (8-10) perfect for executive teams or departments
- All-inclusive pricing simplifies budgeting
- Provence location offers prestige and uniqueness
- Flexible activities accommodate different preferences (art, wellness, culinary)
