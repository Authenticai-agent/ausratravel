Your Perfect Day

Each experience is thoughtfully designed with flexibility built in—join group activities or enjoy your own pace.

Day 1: Arrival & Welcome

Airport pickup, hotel check-in, welcome dinner with your small group. Settle into the Provençal rhythm.

Day 2-3: Immersive Experiences

Morning workshops (art, yoga, meditation, or free time). Afternoon excursions to villages, markets, or nature. Evening wine & cuisine.

Day 4: Farewell & Departure

Final morning activity, farewell lunch, and transfer to Nice airport. Depart with memories and new skills.

Flexible: All activities are opt-in. Enjoy solitude, join group adventures, or mix both—your journey, your way.
